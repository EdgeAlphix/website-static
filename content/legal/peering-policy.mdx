# Peering Policy

## EdgeAlphix Network Peering Policy

EdgeAlphix Network (AS201243) operates as an open peering network and welcomes peering with networks in mutual locations.

## General Policy

Open peering policy. We are willing to establish settlement-free peering sessions at public Internet exchanges where we have presence.

## Requirements

- Valid IRR and RPKI records for announced prefixes
- Proper route filtering and adherence to BCP-38
- 24×7 NOC contact for operational matters

## Peering Locations

Refer to our PeeringDB record for up-to-date locations.
