## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information about site usage.

Cookies may be temporary (session cookies) or persistent (stored until deleted or expired).

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They may:

- Enable navigation and page rendering
- Maintain security and session integrity
- Support basic performance and infrastructure stability

These cookies cannot be disabled without affecting website functionality.

### Performance and Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with the website. These cookies may collect:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referring websites
- Browser and device information

This information is used in aggregated form to improve website performance and reliability.

### Functional Cookies

Functional cookies may remember user preferences, such as interface settings or consent choices, to improve usability.
