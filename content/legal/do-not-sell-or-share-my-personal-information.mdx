# Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

**Effective Date: Feb 25, 2026**

EdgeAlphix LLC ("EdgeAlphix," "we," "our," or "us") does not sell personal information for monetary consideration.

We do not share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes.

## 1. Your Rights

If you are a California resident or reside in a jurisdiction with similar privacy rights, you may have the right to:

- Request disclosure of categories of personal information collected
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of certain data-sharing practices

Although EdgeAlphix does not sell personal information, you may submit a request to confirm your status or exercise applicable privacy rights.

## 2. Submitting a Request

To submit a request related to your personal information, please contact:

contact@edgealphix.com

Please include:

- Your full name
- Email address used in communication with us
- Description of your request

We may require reasonable verification before processing certain requests.

## 3. No Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising applicable privacy rights.

## 4. Authorized Agents

Where permitted by law, authorized agents may submit requests on behalf of an individual, subject to verification requirements.

## 5. Updates

We may update this notice periodically. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.
